About This Game


Play through a near-scratch-replica of WarioWare (which was VERY frustrating to develop given how scratch makes simple tasks overly-complex).

Play through a compilation of 10 micro games, and try to get the highest score possible! The games within vary in terms of function, so this project should provide an enjoyable experience until it becomes overly-repetitive.
